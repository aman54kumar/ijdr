Empirical Research Projects

The institute has completed various empirical projects on micro and pressing issues, contributing significantly to development research across multiple domains.

Child Labour in Eastern Uttar Pradesh

Focus Area: Social Development

A comprehensive case study of the Silk Saree Industry of Varanasi, examining the complex socio-economic factors contributing to child labour practices in the region.

Varanasi Region Industry Study
Marketing Management of Village Pottery

Focus Area: Rural Economy

An in-depth analysis of traditional pottery marketing strategies in Kashi Vidyapith Block, exploring sustainable development opportunities for rural artisans.

Kashi Vidyapith Block Traditional Crafts
Socio-Economic Study of Schedule Caste

Focus Area: Social Equity

A detailed socio-economic analysis of Schedule Caste communities in Varanasi City, examining development challenges and opportunities for inclusive growth.

Varanasi City Social Analysis
Impact of Fertilizer on Agricultural Productivity

Focus Area: Agricultural Development

Comprehensive research examining fertilizer impact on agricultural productivity with special reference to Varanasi District, contributing to sustainable farming practices.

Varanasi District Agriculture
Women Empowerment in Indian Railway

Focus Area: Gender & Employment

An extensive case study of Diesel Locomotive Works, Varanasi, analyzing women's participation in the railway sector and identifying pathways for enhanced gender inclusion in public sector employment.

DLW Varanasi Women Empowerment Public Sector
