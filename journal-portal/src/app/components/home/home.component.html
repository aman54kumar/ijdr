About IJDR

The Indian Journal of Development Research (IJDR) is a peer-reviewed, refereed, interdisciplinary bi-annual journal published by the Institute of Development Studies, Varanasi.

The journal aims to provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and practitioners to share their findings and insights on various aspects of development research.
