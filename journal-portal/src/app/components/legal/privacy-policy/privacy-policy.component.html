Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Indian Journal of Development Research (IJDR) collects information to provide better services to our users. We collect information in the following ways:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account or submit a manuscript, we collect your name, email address, institutional affiliation, and contact details.
  • Research Information: Manuscript submissions, peer review data, and academic credentials.
  • Communication: Records of your communications with our editorial team.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including pages visited and time spent.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and cookies.
  • Analytics: Website performance and user interaction data through Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Publishing Services: To process manuscript submissions, conduct peer reviews, and publish accepted articles.
  • Communication: To send editorial decisions, publication notifications, and journal updates.
  • Website Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our website functionality.
  • Research Integrity: To maintain the quality and integrity of our publication process.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and institutional requirements.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Peer Reviewers: Manuscript details (without revealing author identity) for the review process.
  • Published Articles: Author names and affiliations in published research articles.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process.
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our website and publishing platform.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Staff training on data protection practices

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal and contractual obligations).
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information.
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service.

6. Contact Information
