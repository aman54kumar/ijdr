Open Access Policy

Promoting free and unrestricted access to scientific research

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Our Commitment to Open Access

The Indian Journal of Development Research (IJDR) is committed to the principles of open access and believes that research should be freely available to researchers, practitioners, and the public worldwide. We support the global movement toward open science and knowledge sharing.

2. Open Access Model

Gold Open Access

IJDR follows the Gold Open Access model, where all published articles are immediately and permanently free to access, download, and share.

No Article Processing Charges (APCs)

Currently, IJDR does not charge Article Processing Charges (APCs) to authors. Publishing with IJDR is completely free for authors.

3. License Terms

Creative Commons Licensing

Articles published in IJDR are licensed under Creative Commons licenses:

  • CC BY 4.0: Attribution - allows distribution, remixing, adaptation, and building upon the material in any medium or format, as long as attribution is given to the creator
  • CC BY-NC 4.0: Attribution-NonCommercial - allows distribution and adaptation for non-commercial purposes only
  • CC BY-SA 4.0: Attribution-ShareAlike - requires adaptations to be shared under the same license

4. Author Rights

Under our open access policy, authors retain:

  • Copyright ownership of their work
  • Right to deposit pre-prints and post-prints in institutional repositories
  • Right to share their work on personal websites and academic social networks
  • Right to reuse their work for educational and research purposes
  • Right to include their work in thesis or dissertation

5. Archiving and Preservation

Digital Preservation

IJDR ensures long-term preservation of published content through:

  • Digital archiving in multiple secure repositories
  • Partnership with academic libraries for distributed preservation
  • Regular backup and migration of digital content
  • Persistent identifiers (DOIs) for all published articles

6. Benefits of Open Access

For Authors

  • Increased visibility and citations
  • Global reach and impact
  • Compliance with funder mandates
  • Enhanced collaboration opportunities

For Readers

  • Free access to latest research
  • No subscription barriers
  • Immediate availability upon publication
  • Mobile-friendly access

For Society

  • Accelerated scientific progress
  • Knowledge equity and accessibility
  • Reduced research duplication
  • Support for developing country researchers

7. Repository Policies

Self-Archiving

Authors are encouraged to deposit their work in:

  • Institutional repositories
  • Subject-specific repositories
  • Personal websites
  • Academic social networks (ResearchGate, Academia.edu)

Version Guidelines

  • Pre-print: Can be shared at any time
  • Post-print: Can be shared immediately after acceptance
  • Publisher version: Can be shared immediately with proper attribution

8. Compliance with Mandates

IJDR's open access policy supports compliance with:

  • University and institutional open access mandates
  • Government funding agency requirements
  • International open access policies
  • Research assessment frameworks
