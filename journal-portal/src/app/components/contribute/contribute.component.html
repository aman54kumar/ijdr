Submission Guidelines

Guidelines for Contributors

Join our community of researchers and contribute to the advancement of development studies

Manuscript Requirements

  • Length: 5,000-7,000 words (A4 size, 10 pages)
  • Format: Typed 1.5 spaces, font size 12
  • Font: Times New Roman (English), Kruti Dev size 16 (Hindi)
  • Language: English
  • Abstract: 100-150 words required

Subscription Details

  • India Individual: Rs. 20,000 (per paper per author)
  • International Individual: US$ 40 (per paper per author)
  • Institution (Annual): Rs. 10,000 / US$ 100
  • Lifetime Individual: Rs. 10,000 / US$ 200
  • Institution (Lifetime): Rs. 12,000

Submission Requirements

Please ensure your manuscript includes the title of the paper followed by author name, institutional address, postal address, mobile number, and email. Tables and figures should appear near/after where they are referenced in the text, sequentially numbered (e.g., Table 1, Fig 1). References should be listed at the end with corresponding reference numbers in the main text.

Review Process

  • Peer Review: Double-blind review process
  • Timeline: 4-6 weeks review period
  • Decision: Accept, Revise, or Reject
  • Feedback: Detailed reviewer comments provided

Ethical Standards

  • Originality: No plagiarism or duplicate submission
  • Ethics: Follow research ethics guidelines
  • Citations: Proper attribution required
  • Conflicts: Declare any conflicts of interest
